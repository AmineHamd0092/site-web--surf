About Us

This beautiful surf camp which is just a 2 minute walk away from the beach is an oasis of world travellers and locals alike where you will feel right at home. It has two bungalows which can be shared as well as a private studio. The large outdoor dining area and the rooftop chill out terrace with hammocks is probably where you will spend most of your time, when youâ€™re not on the waves!

Our guests have access to free wifi and a library of books and magazines on surfing and travels. Fresh and delicious breakfast, lunch and dinners homemade with local produce are included in the weekly packages.

Besides surfing, surfing and surfing, you will also love the gorgeous sunsets on the kilometres long beach as well as the morning bird songs thanks to the Sidi Boughaba Natural Reserve right behind Mehdia which is on the migratory route between Europe and Africa of hundreds of species of birds.
